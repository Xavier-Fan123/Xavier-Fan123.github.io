Profile

An operating style built on risk control, market structure, and disciplined execution.

I came to LPG trading from petrochemical risk control, so I read a position from the exposure first and the headline second. My work sits where the physical market, the paper market, and the financing framework all have to serve the same commercial objective.

In practice that means translating physical constraints into pricing decisions, connecting paper exposure back to real flow, and designing finance structure into the trade rather than bolting it on afterwards.

When the tools don't exist, I build them — forward-curve and risk dashboards, research workflows, and trading systems that turn a market view into something executable.
